The Origin

The Hearth of
Hanshin

It began not with a grand vision, but with a single pot simmering on a cold winter night in Seoul. The warmth of the broth drew strangers closer, turning them into confidants.

Today, we recreate that intimacy. Our space is designed to feel like an extension of that original hearth—dark walnut, amber lights, and the clinking of glasses narrating stories untold.
